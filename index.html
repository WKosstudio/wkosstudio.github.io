About us

Indie dev, chaos creator, and “I’ll do it myself” enthusiast.

Hi, I’m Vadym Kostariev, and WKos Studio is the name I work under —
even if it’s just me for now.
I once imagined this whole thing growing into a studio big enough
to make someone at Tencent panic for a moment:
“where did this guy come from?!”

Nobody at Tencent is panicking yet — I’m still the one coding, drawing, designing,
testing, inventing mechanics, and rescheduling deadlines like it’s part of the craft.
But I keep going because I genuinely enjoy making games,
even when common sense quietly disagrees.

This website exists simply because sometimes life hands you a form that says
“please add your website link.”
So, well… here’s the website.
